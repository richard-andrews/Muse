Welcome to Muse

Here you can relax and find your muse.


Explore the sound options below, get comfortable, close your eyes and relax

or interact with the artefact to make it zoom, spin and move. You can click the large arrows to view a different artefact.


Please aim to spend ~10 minutes using Muse to feel the full effect of the experience.
